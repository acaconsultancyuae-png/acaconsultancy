Phase 01 — Concept Design & Client Brief

We begin by understanding your vision, facility type, patient capacity, and regulatory constraints. Our team conducts a thorough site analysis and prepares initial space programming aligned with UAE health authority guidelines.

  • Site Analysis
  • Space Programming
  • Regulatory Checklist
  • Initial Cost Estimate

Phase 02 — Schematic Design (SD)

Preliminary design layouts are developed, incorporating all health authority requirements for room dimensions, circulation, infection control zones, and MEP infrastructure planning.

  • Floor Plan Layouts
  • Infection Control Zoning
  • MEP Coordination
  • Authority Pre-Check

Phase 03 — Detailed Design (DD)

Comprehensive construction drawings are produced to the full specification required by health authorities, including all technical rooms, medical gas systems, and accessibility requirements.

  • Technical Drawings
  • Medical Gas Plans
  • Accessibility Compliance
  • Material Specifications

Phase 04 — Authority Submission

We compile and submit complete application packages to DHA, HAAD, MOHAP, or relevant municipal authorities — managing all correspondence and responding to technical queries on your behalf.

  • DHA Submission
  • MOHAP Application
  • HAAD Compliance
  • Municipality NOC

Phase 05 — Approval & NOC Issuance

We track the review process, respond to authority comments, and coordinate any required design modifications to achieve final approval and No Objection Certificates in the shortest possible timeframe.

  • Comment Resolution
  • Design Amendments
  • NOC Issuance
  • Inspection Coordination

Phase 06 — Handover & Post-Occupancy

Upon completion, we provide as-built documentation, support your accreditation audit readiness, and remain available for any post-occupancy queries or future expansion consultancy.

  • As-Built Drawings
  • O&M Manuals
  • Accreditation Support
  • Future Expansion Advisory
